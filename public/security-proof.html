🛡️ Security Architecture
🛡️

Immutability

Every block holds the hash of the previous block. Altering any record would invalidate every subsequent hash — making tampering computationally impossible.

AlgorithmSHA-256
ConsensusPolygon PoS
🔑

ECDSA Signing

Every data owner (Farmer, Agri Officer, Tahsildar, IAgS) signs their block with their unique ECDSA key pair, ensuring non-repudiation and data ownership.

Curvesecp256k1
HashKeccak-256
📜

Smart Contracts

Business rules are enforced by autonomous smart contracts on Polygon Amoy — no human can bypass the certification, price-lock, or relief-fund approval workflow.

NetworkPolygon Amoy
LanguageSolidity 0.8
🔍

QR Audit Trail

Each batch QR code points to a full on-chain audit trail readable by anyone — Farmer → Agri Officer → Retailer → Consumer, with timestamps and txHashes.

FormatURL → /trace/:batchId
LevelQR Level H
🌿

Price Lock (±0.2%)

The Agri Officer cannot adjust farmer's quoted price by more than ±0.2%. This rule is enforced at the API and smart-contract level to protect farmer income.

Max Δ±0.2%
EnforcedAPI + Chain
📍

Location-Based Access

Agri Officers can only inspect crops from their own registered district and taluk, preventing cross-region data manipulation. This is enforced server-side.

ScopeDistrict + Taluk
EnforcedJWT + MongoDB
